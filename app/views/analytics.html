OVERVIEW
  • Used AngularJS, D3JS, PhantomJS, jQuery, ChartJS, PDFKitJS, HTML5, and CSS3 to generate reports that would amass statistics regarding the organization.
  • Utilized a Rails backend to retrieve data from PostgreSQL database, and used sorting methods to organize the data.
  • Made use of JavaScript's asynchronous functions to render charts in a headless browser using PhantomJS.
  • Created a rake task paired with a CRON tab to schedule and automate the mailing of the outputs.
  • Decrypted identifiable information using RSA-AES encryption.
    • SUMMARY
    My primary role at HealthIM was improving analytics and reporting for mental health crisis calls. One key tool I developed was the reporting/output tool that is used by hospital administration, community mental health organizations, and police services. For hospital and community mental health organizations, the tool amasses and organizes data to display relevant statistics regarding the patient and their history. On the other hand, for policing administration, it acts as a valuable tool for tracking a detachment's effectiveness against mental health crisis calls.
