OVERVIEW
  • Used AngularJS, jQuery, ChartJS, HTML5, and CSS3 to produce a seamless interface to display client information and risk history to hospital and policing administration.
  • Encrypted all data in transit using military-grade RSA-AES Encryption, and used a PostgreSQL database.
  • Utilized a Rails backend to configure routes to efficiently retrieve and post data to the server.
    • SUMMARY
    Case Management is my most recent project with my previous company, HealthIM. It is a tool used by both hospital and police administration to perform history lookups on individuals who are frequently apprehended due to mental health crises. Since the data displayed is sensitive information, all identifiable information in transit is encrypted/decrypted using RSA/AES encryption. The tool allows administration to view an individual's previous apprehensions, analyze whether their at risk for another apprehension, and add sensitive information to their case file.
